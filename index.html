Welcome to R.D. College, Sheikhpura [Bihar]

Ramadhin College, Sheikhpura was established in 1969. It was established by Late Sri. Rajo Singh, a member of Parliament, a visionary, mass leader and the creator of Sheikhpura District, with the help of intellectuals, social activist & common people of Sheikhpura. Sri Rajo Singh was the founder secretary of the college and continued to the post till the conversion of the college into a constituent unit of T. M. Bhagalpur University, Bhagalpur. Ramadhin College was named after the name of Shri Ramadhin Singh, village - Abgil, P.S. - Sheikhpura, who was a man of wisdom, a social thinker and a great affluent farmer. His contribution in the field of knowledge is immense and setup a milestone in education. The college become a constituent unit of T.M. Bhagalpur University, Bhagalpur on 12th of December, 1980....
